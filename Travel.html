Bihar Ka Itihaas, Prakriti Ki Goad Me

Explore the ancient ruins of Nalanda University & the scenic hills of Rajgir

Famous Places to Visit

Nalanda University Ruins

World Heritage Site. 5th century ki prachin university. UNESCO site hai.

Ticket: ₹40 Indian

Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Rajgir

Ropeway se pahadi pe. White marble ka shandar stupa. Full city view.

Ropeway: ₹120

Brahmakund Hot Springs

Natural hot water kund. Medicinal properties. Male/Female alag.

Timing: 5 AM - 9 PM

Ghora Katora Lake

Pahadi se ghira shant lake. Boating hoti hai. Buddha statue 70ft.

Boating: ₹50

Itihaas Ke Panne Se

Nalanda University

5th century me establish hui thi. World ki pehli residential university. 10,000 students + 2000 teachers the. Bakhtiyar Khilji ne 1193 me destroy kar diya tha.

Rajgir

Magadh ki pehli capital. Buddha aur Mahavir dono yahan aaye the. Jarasandh ka Akhara bhi yahin hai. Ajatashatru ka kila ke ruins bhi hain.

Kaise Pahunche?

By Train

Nearest: Rajgir Railway Station. Patna se 3hr. Gaya se 1.5hr.

By Bus

Patna, Gaya, Bihar Sharif se direct bus. BSRTC chalti hai.

By Air

Nearest Airport: Gaya 70km, Patna 100km.
