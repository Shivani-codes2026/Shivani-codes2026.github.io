💠Famous Places to Visit✨

➡️

💁‍♀Nalanda University Ruins👩‍🎓

World Heritage Site. 5th century ki prachin university. UNESCO site hai.

Ticket: ₹40 Indian

💁‍♀Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Rajgir⛲

Ropeway se pahadi pe. White marble ka shandar stupa. Full city view.

Ropeway: ₹120

💁‍♀Brahmakund Hot Springs🌊🔥

Natural hot water kund. Medicinal properties. Male/Female alag.

Timing: 5 AM - 9 PM

💁‍♀Ghora Katora Lake🦄🏞

Pahadi se ghira shant lake, jo ghore ke akar ki dikhne vali ak prakritik jhil h, isliye iska name ghora katora h. Yahan Boating hoti h,or iske center me 70ft kids Buddha statue h.

Boating: ₹50

💠Itihaas Ke Panne Se📖

➡️

💁‍♀Nalanda University🎗

5th century me establish hui thi. World ki pehli residential university.Isme 10,000 students👨‍🎓 + 2000 teachers👨‍🏫 the, Bakhtiyar Khilji ne 1193 (12vin shatabdi) me apne sainiko se poori university me aag 🔥lagva diya, is aag se nalanda ki vishal library me rakhi pandulipiyan 📚( 90 lac books) destroy 🔥 kar diya tha.

💁‍♀Rajgir✨

Magadh ki pehli capital. Buddha aur Mahavir dono yahan aaye the. Jarasandh ka Akhara🤼‍♂ bhi yahin hai. Ajatashatru ka kila ke ruins bhi hain, yahan vibhinn prakar ke visit places h or adhik sankhya me yahan bandaro 🐒k jhund paye jate h.
